Services

Whether you’re starting from scratch or looking to expand your online presence, it’s essential that each channel of any marketing campaign is closely aligned with and strategically working towards the same final goal: reaching customers.

Take a look at the various services we offer to learn how Ivy Digital’s unique approach to digital marketing can help your company.

Conversion Optimization

Having traffic on your website shouldn’t be your only end goal. You need to make sure that your website traffic converts into potential customers.

Social Media Marketing

If you are not targeting social media for business, you will inevitably fail to address a large number of audience. We provide highly-effective social media profiling, competition analysis and engagement.

Paid Search Management

Is organic search becoming a problem for your business? Well, not anymore. Our PPC experts will do the right budgeting for your keywords and make sure that your website content ranks.

Who I am

I am a Google certified and seasoned marketer with over 12 years of digital marketing experience from Amazon, IBM, Tableau, and Ernst & Young, with a university degree from Cornell and an MBA from Yale. This background has given me the unique ability to successfully manage digital marketing activities, while owning analytical aspects as well.
